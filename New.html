Drake's son's 7th birthday looked like "the best day ever!"

(By Bailey Richards)

The 37-year-old rapper’s son Adonis Graham turned 7 on Friday, Oct. 11, and he celebrated by throwing what appears to be a SpongeBob SquarePants-themed bash — and sharing father-son snaps.

On Sunday, Oct. 13, the “One Dance” rapper (born Aubrey Drake Graham) marked the 7-year-old’s latest milestone with a sweet Instagram post. Drake shared two photos, featuring himself and Adonis, backdropped by a Bikini Bottom-inspired setting, including jellyfish and the cartoon’s flower-shaped clouds. SpongeBob-inspired text read, “Adonis’ 7th Birthday.”
